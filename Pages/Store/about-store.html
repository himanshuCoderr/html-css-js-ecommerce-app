About TeacherOn Store


The TeacherOn Store is a great place to sell notes, lesson plans, worksheets, presentations, research
papers, digital activities, and other educational tools.


Why sell at TeacherOn Store?

  • Passive income from resources you have already made during your tutoring career
    You probably wrote notes during your studies, made presentations to your students, etc. You can sell it in the store.
  • Having products in the store boosts your tutor profile
    Students and parents will see your products and want you to be their tutor, and you can command more for your services. You can share products for free for more exposure.
  • Reach a variety of teachers, students, and parents
    Sell your material to people from around the world as you sleep. Expand your impact by reaching a vast network of parents, students, and fellow educators, amplifying your influence and earnings.

What to consider when uploading your product.


  1. Write a clear title and description so buyers understand what you are selling. People usually
    don't buy when in doubt.
  2. Upload preview files so people can see what they will get.
  3. Ensure that the complete solution is uploaded in the "Solution Files" section.



Why buy at TeacherOn Store?


  • Teachers from around the world
    This variety ensures that you will find culturally relevant, diverse, and innovative resources.
  • The resources can be used both online and offline
    They are perfect for teaching physical classrooms, virtual classrooms, or a hybrid of the two.
  • There are also free materials

  • Feedback and peer reviews mean you can see which resources are best
    These ensure continuous improvement of the resources, so they stay up to date and are getting better all the time.
